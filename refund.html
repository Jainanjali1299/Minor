The refund policy

Cancellation Policy:If you cancel more than 24 hours before pickup, you'll likely receive a full refund ¹ However, if you cancel within 24 hours, you may be charged a cancellation fee or lose a portion of your prepaid amount ¹ ².

Refund Methods:Refunds are usually processed to the original payment method, such as credit or debit cards ¹ ². Some companies may also offer refunds via cash or check ².

Timeframe for Refunds:Refunds can take around 5-10 business days to process, depending on the company and your bank's policies ².

Additional Fees: Keep in mind that additional fees, such as fuel charges, excess mileage fees, or damage charges, may be deducted from your refund ¹ ³.
